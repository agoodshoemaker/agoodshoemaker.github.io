## [time budget]

Continuing from where I left off previously on the <a href="/notes/2019-10-07-notes.html" target="blank">$7^{th}$ October</a>.

- exploration-assimilation
- What is a project - anything that you engage with actively becomes a project. The Apollo-11 videos became a project the moment you started Ankifying them. You no longer watch it passively. 90% of the time should be devoted to projects; 10% for exploration. While working on projects you assimilate; while exploring you look for potential projects. For example, juggling is a potential project.
- In your explorations, you may come across content relevant to existing projects or you may come across content that may become potential projects. These need to be stored somewhere.
