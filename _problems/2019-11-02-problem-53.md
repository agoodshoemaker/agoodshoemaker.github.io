Four circles of equal radius are packed into a unit square such that there is no overlap between any pair of circles and the circles are contained entirely inside the square. A particular arrangement of circles is more efficient than another if it covers a greater area of the unit square. What should be the radius of the circles and their arrangement to ensure greatest efficiency? What is that efficiency?
