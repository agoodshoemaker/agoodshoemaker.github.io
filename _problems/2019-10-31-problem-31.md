**(AMTI-2004-SubJunior-Finals)** $A$ is a set of $2004$ positive integers. Show that there is a pair of elements in $A$ whose difference is divisible by $2003$.
<!--more-->

<hr><br>

The remainder when a number is divided by $2003$ can range from $0$ to $2002$, both included. So $2003$ remainders are possible. As the set $A$ has $2004$ numbers, at least two numbers must have the same remainder. The difference of these two numbers must be divisible by $2003$.
